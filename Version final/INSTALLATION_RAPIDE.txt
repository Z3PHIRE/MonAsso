MonAsso - Installation rapide (Windows)

Option 1 (portable, recommandee)
1) Ouvrez le dossier MonAsso-v1.0.0\
2) Lancez Lancer-MonAsso.bat

Option 2 (archive unique)
1) Dezippez MonAsso-v1.0.0-portable.zip
2) Lancez Lancer-MonAsso.bat

Le coeur applicatif est:
MonAsso-v1.0.0\MonAsso\MonAsso.exe
